Event Date: Saturday 28th April, 2023 | the event will be attended by exceptional speakers from the world of business, governance, angel investors and VC’s.

SU.X.SS, is a one-day conclave that will bring together some of the best minds in sustainable entrepreneurship together. It will put the spotlight on startups and founders who have balanced the wellbeing of mother earth with the need to generate wealth.

The conclave will bring together entrepreneurs who have contributed towards the sustainability of our world and who run their businesses with ‘innovative manner’, to speak, to encourage and mentor other companies looking to adopt a sustainable form of trade.

About Us

Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) RAISE is India's 1st startup incubation Centre focusing on United Nations Sustainable Development Goals by delivering solutions through start-ups to environmental problems. We work on nurturing and supporting start-ups that use technology and creativity to solve social and environmental problems.

We support mission-driven founders and start-ups by providing all necessary support to achieve their mission. We are part of the prestigious Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) and NITI Aayog, Government of India.
